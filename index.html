What we do

Integrated research and strategy services

Yug Logic combines ground operations, digital intelligence and rigorous analysis to deliver a single, coherent picture for decision‑makers.

A. Feedback Collection
Structured voices, not scattered opinions

We gather authentic feedback from voters and customers through both online and offline channels. Our proprietary system enables deep, real-time outreach to sources, capturing micro-level developments and behavioural changes as they occur.

  • Digital forms, telephonic outreach and on-ground interactions.
  • Segment-wise respondent profiling without compromising privacy.
  • Alerting mechanism for emerging issues and sentiments.
B. Market Research, Data Analysis & Voter Mapping
From raw signals to clear patterns

We analyse trending search keywords, social media conversations, structured feedback forms, and person-to-person interactions at the grassroots level. By mapping public opinion, market dynamics, and future trends, we design precise interventions.

  • Constituency‑wise voter mapping and micro‑segmentation.
  • Sentiment and priority mapping across time and regions.
  • Door-to-door campaigns and targeted outreach planning.
C. Political Services
End‑to‑end election campaign architecture

We provide full‑spectrum election campaign strategy, grounded in data and disciplined execution.

  • Comprehensive voter-list analysis and booth strategy.
  • War-room operations and rapid response units.
  • Social media image building and reputation management.
  • Door-to-door campaign planning and execution.
  • Multi-level team coordination and reporting structures.
  • Speech drafting for rallies and media appearances.
  • Ground‑level constituency issue mapping and real‑time opinion tracking.
D. Policy Consultancy & Governance Solutions
Evidence‑based governance design

We offer expert consultancy in policy formulation, problem‑solving, succession planning, and grievance redressal system design, rooted in robust research.

  • Stakeholder mapping and consultative research.
  • Evidence‑based strategy development.
  • Pilot project implementation and refinement.
  • Predictive impact assessment before full‑scale rollout.
E. Advertising, Branding & Social Media Management
Narratives that travel to the right audience

We create impactful advertising campaigns, manage brand positioning, and run targeted social media strategies to reach the right audience at the right time with the right message.

  • Brand story and identity development.
  • Integrated campaign planning across channels.
  • Performance monitoring and creative optimisation.
